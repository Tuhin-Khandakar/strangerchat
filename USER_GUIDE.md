# STRNGR - User Guide

Welcome to **STRNGR**, a simple, safe, and anonymous chat platform. Connect with random strangers instantly without creating a profile or giving up your privacy.

## Getting Started

1.  **Open the App**: Navigate to `https://strngr.chat` (or your deployment URL).
2.  **Agree to Terms**: Check the box confirming you are 18+ and agree to our rules.
3.  **Start Chatting**: Click **"Start Chatting"**.
4.  **Connect**: You will be instantly matched with a stranger. Say "Hi"!

## Features

*   **Totally Anonymous**: No login, no profiles, no history.
*   **Text Only**: Focus on conversation, not appearances.
*   **Mobile Friendly**: Works great on your phone or desktop.
*   **Safe**: Automated moderation helps keep the chat clean.

## Controls

*   **Next**: Skip the current conversation and find a new partner immediately.
*   **Report**: If someone is being abusive, click the "Report" button. This helps us ban bad actors.
*   **theme Toggle**: Switch between Light and Dark mode using the sun/moon icon.

## FAQ

**Q: Is it really anonymous?**
A: Yes. We don't store your IP address permanently, and we don't ask for your name or email.

**Q: Can I find a previous chat partner?**
A: No. Once you disconnect, that connection is gone forever.

**Q: What if I see something inappropriate?**
A: Please use the **Report** button immediately. Our system learns from these reports to ban offenders.

**Q: Why "STRNGR"?**
A: Because every friend starts as a stranger.
