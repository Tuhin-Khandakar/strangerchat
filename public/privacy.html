Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

StrangerChat is designed to be anonymous. We do not require registration or personal data like names or email addresses. However, we process your IP address for security and moderation purposes.

2. IP Hashing

To enforce bans without storing raw IP addresses, we use cryptographic hashing (SHA-256). This allows us to identify banned users while protecting your actual IP address in our database.

3. Chat Logs

Chat messages are transmitted in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers once a session ends, unless flagged for manual review via reports.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. Essential session data may be stored temporarily to maintain your connection.
