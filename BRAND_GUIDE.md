# STRNGR Brand Identity & Usage Rules

## The Vision

**STRNGR** is a modern anonymous chat platform focused on real conversations — not chaos. We provide a space for humans to connect without the pressure of profiles, the noise of algorithms, or the permanence of social media.

## Voice & Tone

- **Calm**: No flashy alerts or intrusive pop-ups.
- **Respectful**: System messages are direct and professional.
- **Human**: We speak to users as individuals, not data points.
- **Minimal**: Zero bullshit. Every pixel counts.

### System Message Examples

- `Looking for someone...`
- `You’re connected.`
- `Stranger left the chat.`
- `Report submitted. Thanks for helping keep things safe.`

## Logo Usage Rules

The STRNGR icon represents stability and connection.

- **Padding**: Minimum 24px padding around the icon at all times.
- **No Distortion**: Never stretch, rotate, or shear the logo.
- **No Text**: Never add text inside the icon.
- **Consistency**: Use the navy blue circle with white 'S' for dark backgrounds and the blue 'S' for light backgrounds.

## Typography

- **Font Family**: Inter (Primary & Only)
- **Rules**:
  - Headings: Semi-bold
  - Body: Regular
  - No fancy displays or script fonts.

## Colors

- **Navy (Primary Dark)**: `#0F172A`
- **Blue (Action)**: `#2563EB`
- **Light (Background)**: `#F8FAFC`
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`

## Launch Strategy (Version 1)

- **Minimal Landing**: High impact, clear value prop.
- **Trust Strip**: Reassurance via transparency.
- **How It Works**: 3 simple steps to eliminate friction.
- **No Noise**: No ads, no gamification, no feature bloat.
